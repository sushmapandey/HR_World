Welcome to hr World

One of the noteworthy features of the Indian workplace is a demographic uniqueness.Human Resource Management has evolved rapidly leading the vision for organizational capabilities, developing strategies for talent acquisition, and efficiently managing payroll, recruitment and other functions, leading the business into the future. It has moved from an operational discipline towards a more strategic discipline.

The HR WORLD aims to be India’s leading HR summit focused on the transformed roles in Human Resource Management bringing together 120+ top HR leaders from across industry verticals. This event is more than just a showcase of vendors. It is one of the most interactive, informative, and exciting conferences in the field of HR. It is where those shaping the future of work and technology in Human resources (from large enterprises to the next innovators) network and share ideas.

LEARN

From the industry experts via case studies, solution showcase and panels, followed by vibrant interactive discussions.

CONNECT

DISCOVER

NETWORK

Why Attend

The HR WORLD aims to build a close-knit community of Human Resources Innovators sharing best practices with professionals from across industries.
